Frequently asked questions.

Short answers before you apply. No 47-page partner agreement with PowerPoint perfume.

Do I need to know web development?

No. You do not need to build websites. You only need to refer relevant customers and explain the offer honestly.

What does the customer get?

The customer gets 10% off the first order and can receive a first demo before buying.

What do I get as an affiliate?

You get 20% commission on the first paid order from a new customer who comes through your personal link or discount code.

How do you know who referred the customer?

Each approved affiliate gets a personal referral link and discount code. That makes it clear where the customer came from.

When is commission paid?

After the customer has paid and the order has been approved. Exact payout method is agreed after your application is approved.

Do old customers count?

No. The program applies to new customers where your link or code has actually been used.

Can I create my own marketing material?

Yes, but it must be accurate and must not promise things that are not true. I can also help with short texts if needed.

Is this employment?

No. This is affiliate/referral commission. No fixed salary, no employment and no guaranteed income.

Which customers should I refer?

Small businesses, associations, local companies, solo founders, consultants and service providers that need a better website, landing page or web fix.

Can customers outside Sweden use the service?

Yes. I can work remotely and handle projects in Swedish, English, Danish and Norwegian.

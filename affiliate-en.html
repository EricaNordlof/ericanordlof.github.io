The right customer to refer.

A good referral is not “everyone with internet”. A good referral is a person or business where the need already exists.

Best customer

A customer with a real need

  • Has an old or missing website
  • Wants more enquiries
  • Needs a clearer landing page
  • Can make or influence the decision
  • Has reasonable expectations
Wrong customer

Do not refer just to refer.

  • People who only want to “look around” without a need
  • People who expect free work
  • Customers who are not relevant for the service
  • Customers who expect things that are not offered
  • Customers who think you handle delivery
Good signals

Listen for this.

  • “I should fix the website.”
  • “We need more customers.”
  • “Our website is old.”
  • “We only have Facebook right now.”
  • “We need something simple and clear.”

Frequently asked questions.

Short answers before you apply. No 47-page partner attachment smelling of PowerPoint.

Do I have to sell?

No. You can simply refer someone or make an introduction. I take the customer dialogue from there.

Do I need to know web development?

No. You do not need to build websites yourself. You only need to refer relevant customers and explain the offer honestly.

Am I responsible if the customer is not satisfied?

No. You are not responsible for delivery, support, customer contact, quote, project work or follow-up.

What does the customer get?

The customer gets 10% discount through your personal code and can get a first demo before purchase when the project is a good fit.

What do I get as a referrer?

You get 20% referral commission if the customer you referred from the beginning leads to an approved and paid project.

Can I get commission for an existing customer?

No. Commission only applies to customers you actually brought in from the beginning and who were not already in dialogue with Nordlöf Nordic before your referral. Existing customers cannot be registered afterwards as referred customers.

How do you know who referred the customer?

Every approved referrer gets a personal referral code or referral link. The customer can also name you as the referrer in the dialogue.

When do I get paid?

After the customer has paid and the project is approved under the terms. Exact payment method is decided when your application is approved.

Can I post in groups?

Yes, if the group rules allow it and the post is relevant. No spam, no mass posting and no false promises.

Can I create my own promotional material?

Yes, but it must be accurate and must not promise things that are not true. I can also help with short texts if needed.

Is this employment?

No. This is referral commission. No fixed salary, no employment and no guaranteed income.

Is this the main sales channel?

No. Direct customer contact is still the main track. The referral setup is an extra channel for relevant introductions.

Which customers should I refer?

Small businesses, associations, local companies, sole traders, consultants and service businesses that need a better website, landing page or web fix.

Can customers outside the Nordics use the service?

Yes. I can work remotely with customers in any country, as long as the project can be done in Swedish, English, Danish or Norwegian. English works for many international customers.

Target audience

Make it clear who the page is for.

Here you describe which customers the company fits best. It filters out the wrong leads and makes the right visitor feel safer.

Write simply, concretely and without fluff. A visitor should feel: this is for me.

    Describe a third target group, industry or situation.
Accessibility options in the final version

Want to make the page easier to use for more people? That can be built in.

In the final version, every page can include accessibility options if you want that. For example: language options, short summary reading, high contrast and larger text. Nothing is mandatory — but it is useful when the target audience needs faster, clearer and easier-to-read information.

🌍

Language options

Swedish, English, Danish, Norwegian or another language can be added depending on your customers.

🔊

Short reading

A button can read a short page summary for visitors who prefer listening instead of reading.

◐

High contrast

The visitor can switch to a clearer contrast view when readability matters more than decoration.

A+

Larger text

A simple button can enlarge the text on the page. The glasses can take a break; the page does the job.

Frequently asked questions.

Answers to what customers often want to know before sending a request.

What do you build?

I build coded websites, landing pages, website fixes, contact forms, quote forms, booking flows, APIs, integrations, backend solutions and simple web apps.

How fast do I get a demo?

When the project fits the demo-first setup, you normally get a first demo within 1 business day after sending the material.

Do I have to buy if I get a demo?

No. You only buy if you like the direction and want to move forward. If it does not feel right, you say no.

Why are the prices low right now?

I am new as a business owner and I am building portfolio, customer base and references. That is why the price is lower right now, but the setup is still serious and clear.

Do you work in WordPress?

I mainly build new websites from scratch in code. WordPress is not the default setup. It makes the solution more flexible and reduces unnecessary lock-in.

Can you build API, backend or web apps?

Yes. Larger or more technical solutions are normally handled hourly, 650 SEK/hour. This can include APIs, integrations, databases, login, form flows or simple internal systems.

What do I need to send to get a good demo?

Describe what the business does, what audience you want to reach, what the customer should do on the page and preferably link to pages you like or competitors. The clearer the input, the better the demo.

Will I be locked in to you?

No. After purchase, you can get access to material, login details and code if you later want to continue yourself or with someone else.

Can you help companies outside Sweden?

Yes. Projects can be done in Swedish, English, Danish and Norwegian. International projects are done in English.

Is SEO included?

SEO basics are included in the website packages: structure, titles, meta description, mobile adaptation and clear pages. More extensive SEO structure for several services, locations or target groups can be built separately.

What does hosting cost?

For simple coded websites, I can normally host the site without any extra monthly cost. More advanced solutions with backend, database, API, login, payment flows or external integrations may require paid hosting or separate running costs.

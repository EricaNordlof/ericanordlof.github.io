Frequently asked questions.

Answers to what customers often want to know before sending a request.

Is the demo free?

Yes, when the project fits within the demo-first scope. The demo is a concrete preview of direction, structure, message and possible solution. It is not a finished free project.

What happens after the demo?

If you like the direction, we agree on scope, price and next steps before I complete the final work. You should know what you are buying before we move forward.

Do I have to buy if I get a demo?

No. If it does not feel right, we stop without drama. But the demo is not meant for unlimited free versions.

Do I own the website?

Yes. After purchase, you can get code, files, material and relevant assets. You should not be locked in to me.

Do you work in WordPress?

I primarily build in code. This makes the solution more flexible, faster to adapt and easier to move. WordPress is therefore not my default setup.

Can you build API, backend or web apps?

Yes. Larger or more technical solutions are normally handled at 650 SEK/hour or by separate quote. This can include APIs, integrations, databases, login, form flows or simple internal systems.

Why are the prices lower than many others?

I am a newly trained web developer and currently building portfolio, customer base and references. You get a lower price right now, but the work is serious and handled with clear scope.

What does hosting cost?

For simple coded websites, I can normally host the site without any extra monthly cost. More advanced solutions with backend, database, API, login, payment flows or external integrations may require paid hosting or separate running costs.

Is SEO included?

SEO basics are included in the website packages: structure, titles, meta description, mobile adaptation and clear pages. More extensive SEO structure for several services, locations or target groups can be built separately.

Can you help companies outside Sweden?

Yes. Projects can be done in Swedish, English, Danish and Norwegian. International projects are done in English.
